Meet the Author

Chrislyn R. Herring

Chrislyn R. Herring hails from a small town in South Texas, where her passion for writing and storytelling began. Drawing inspiration from literary giants like J.R.R. Tolkien and contemporary masters such as Sarah J. Maas, she pursued her craft with a degree in English Literature from Texas A&M University.

Today she writes from the vibrant city of Austin, Texas, surrounded by friends and family who bring new inspiration to her work every day. When she isn't writing in coffee shops, Chrislyn can be found in the climbing gym, trying new cuisine, or out in nature.
