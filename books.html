Dark Fantasy Epic Adventure Dystopian Survival

Driven by desperation, Elena is on the brink of selling herself to the Den — a grim fate where bodies are commodities for the elite. Her panic sends her sprinting toward the Door, and in a twist of fate it swings open, allowing Elena and Bren a taste of freedom in the outside world. But before they reach the forest's protection, they are shot down and dragged into the clutches of Hell's Keep, beneath the minister's residence.

Bren is thrust into a perilous alliance with the minister, forced to serve as his assassin to keep Elena alive. Faced with sacrificing his humanity to preserve her life, he forms unexpected bonds with fellow assassins Klaus and Thomas, blurring the lines between friend and foe — until the minister's revelation threatens to reshape their world entirely.

Believing her sister dead and her mother condemned to the Red Wall, Alasea embarks on a quest for survival, guided by a cryptic mention of a Book that holds the key to her family's history and the city's blood-woven past.
